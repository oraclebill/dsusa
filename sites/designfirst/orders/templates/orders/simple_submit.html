Order submission

Be sure to select the correct product - Pro Design or Presentation Pack - and processing options - e.g. RUSH Service - for your needs. Each of these options affects pricing so be sure you have enough credit in your account for your selections.

Payment

If you have enough account credit, your order will be submitted as soon as you click 'Submit'. If your design costs more than you have in account credit, clicking submit wil place your order on hold and redirect you to our online shoppign cart. Once you have purchased sufficient credit, you can select your order from the Dashboard and re-submit it for processing.

Processing Time

Standard processing time is two business days. Expedited processing is available for a nominal fee. If you would like expedited service, select the 'RUSH' processing option.

Clarification Process

Our clarification process lets the designer contact you with questions to resolve ambiguities and request additional information about your request.
